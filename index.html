About
What We Do

we partner with musicians to create one-of-a-kind generative art pieces on the cardano blockchain. Holding our art provides access to special events, discounts, and other surprises.

How It's Made

we create algorithms that can generate art by listening to music we play for them. we feed these algorithms songs from our partner musicians to create unique art each and every time.

Each piece uses a variety of random attributes to determine how the algorithm will construct the art. Some attributes are rarer than others.

12 Epics

there will be a total of 7150 dancing pixels. These will be released in a series of 8 epics. Each epic will increase in total supply and price to mint. Epic001 will start at 20 ADA and consist of 100 cnfts. Each epic will increase in price by 10 ADA and the number of cnft's released in each drop will also increases.

the price of the earliest epic held at the time of minting future dancing pixels will be the price paid to mint. For example, holding a dancing pixels cnft from epic001 will allow minting dancing pixels cnfts from all future epics for 30 ada, regardless of their rising price.
